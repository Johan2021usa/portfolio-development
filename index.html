about me

Hi, I'm Johan Lasso, let me tell you a little about my history, I won a scholarship in 2021 to study software development (Associate Degree) in Sergio Arboleda University (Bogota-Colombia).
In that process, I met some people who were already working as a software developer, and then, I started to work for them on real projects, gaining thereby, experience and proficiency in back-end and front-end development.
At the same time, It was a significant opportunity to apply the obtained knowledge during the scholarship, working on software life cycle and implementing SCRUM as an agile methodology for projects of software development.
Subsequently, after finishing the scholarship in 2022, I worked as a bilingual customer service agent for 7 months, where I enhanced my English skills by speaking to american people and solving technical issues. After that, I started to gain more experience in web development working as a freelancer by creating responsive websites, providing high quality products and solving real issues.
Last but not least, currently in 2023 I'm still working as a freelancer developer and also studying an associate degree called "Programming of Applications and Cloud Services" in Servicio Nacional de Aprendizaje (SENA).
